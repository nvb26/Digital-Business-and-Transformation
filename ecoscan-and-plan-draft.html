The Problem

Sustainable shopping shouldn't be this hard

Consumers want to make better choices, but the current shopping experience makes it nearly impossible to know what's truly sustainable.

😵

Confusing information

Dozens of eco-labels, vague claims, and contradictory information make it impossible to compare products quickly and confidently.

⏳

No time to research

Busy students and young professionals care about sustainability but don't have time to google every product while standing in the aisle.

🔍

No in-store guidance

There is no simple, real-time tool available in supermarkets that helps consumers identify sustainable products at a glance.

How It Works

Three steps to a sustainable lifestyle

EcoScan & Plan turns complex sustainability data into instant, actionable insights — right when you need them.

1

Scan Products

Point your camera at any product barcode in the supermarket and get an instant sustainability score from A to E.

2

Plan Meals

Get personalized weekly meal plans based on your preferences, dietary needs, and sustainability goals.

3

Shop Smart

Receive an auto-generated shopping list with the most sustainable product alternatives for your local store.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower every consumer to make sustainable grocery choices effortlessly — by turning complex product data into clear, instant, and actionable insights through AI technology.

Our Vision

A world where sustainable shopping is the default — where every consumer can scan, plan, and buy with confidence, making a positive impact with every purchase.

Business Model

How we create and capture value

📈 Revenue Streams

Freemium subscriptions — Free basic scanning; premium plans (meal planner, advanced analytics) at €3.99/month

B2B partnerships — Supermarkets pay for in-store integration and sustainability analytics dashboards

Brand promotions — Sustainable brands pay for featured visibility in alternative suggestions

Data insights — Anonymized consumer sustainability trend reports for FMCG companies

🛠 Key Resources & Costs

Product database — Licensed from Open Food Facts + proprietary data partnerships

AI/ML infrastructure — Cloud computing for real-time product analysis and meal planning

Development team — Mobile app development, backend engineering, data science

Marketing — Student ambassador programs, social media, supermarket partnerships
